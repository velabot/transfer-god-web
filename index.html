Twitter to Telegram Bot

Receive the tweets of your favourite Twitter accounts. Follow the best accounts to receive updates every hour directly to your Telegram app.

Connect here

Usage

/start
Start receiving tweets.
You are going to receive tweets from the accounts you follow. Only once every hour.
If you paused the service, you can restart with this command.
/stop
Stop receiving tweets. All the accounts you follow are deleted.
/pause
Pause receiving tweets.
Restart with /start command.
/follow <account name>
Start following an account.
Don't type @.
Sample: "/follow elonmusk".
/unfollow <account name>
Start following an account.
Don't type @.
Sample: "/unfollow elonmusk".
/list
Show a list of the account you follow.
/help or /menu
Show this help.
